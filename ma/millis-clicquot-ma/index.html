Millis Clicquot, MA Insights: Population, Influence, and Service Areas

Millis-Clicquot is a census-designated place (CDP) in the town of Millis in Norfolk County, Massachusetts, United States. The population was 4,403 at the 2010 census. The name 'Clicquot' comes from the now-defunct Clicquot Club Company, a beverage manufacturer that was founded in the area in 1881.
